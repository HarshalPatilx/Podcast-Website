About H-Cast

Full Mutuality provides written and podcast content designed to shine a light on inequalities in various areas of society, particularly in Evangelical Christianity. Hosted by Gail and Nate, the Full Mutuality podcast features conversations about these inequalities and explores ways that justice can be brought into spaces where oppression, marginalization, and inequality run rampant. Both former Evangelical ministers, Gail and Nate share a unique perspective as having been fully devoted adherents while slowly coming to the realization that certain groups of people are left without a seat at the table.
